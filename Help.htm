MAILING LABELS - Tacoma Community & Economic Development Department
  To start the program go to https://wsitd03/website/labels/

  Data Limits: https://wsitd03/website/labels/DataLimits.htm

  Questions? Contact Mike Murnane, Tacoma Community and Economic Development, 591-5293 or mmurnane@cityoftacoma.org

  Quick Overview (31 seconds):
  
STEP 1: GO TO STARTING AREA
 Option 1 - By address or parcel number:

  Use search box (upper left) to zoom to location:

        Intersections can be located by separating the cross streets with an "&"
             Example: Market St & 9th St
    Minimum requirements to locate an intersection include the two street names with at least one street type (usually ST or AV), and a street direction (N, S, E, or NE) if more than one (S 29th ST and N 29th ST) exists.
     
    For Tacoma the directions include: 
     
N - north of 6th Ave & Division Ave
S - south of 6th Ave & Division Ave and west of A ST
E - east of A St and south of the Hylebos Waterway
NE - north of the Hylebos Waterway
    Example: Puget Sound Ave & N 29th St

 Option 2 - By moving the map to the area:
  • CENTER & ZOOM IN - Double Click
  • PAN - Drag the mouse with button pressed
  • RECENTER - Shift + Click
  • ZOOM IN - Mouse scroll forward
  • ZOOM IN (rubber band) - Shift + Drag the mouse
  • ZOOM OUT - Mouse scroll backward
  • ZOOM OUT (rubber band) - Shift + Ctrl + Drag
STEP 2: SELECT AND RUN MAILING LABEL TASK
 Click button to open the mailing label report options panel (if not already open).

 Option 1 - By drawing on the map:
  1. Choose one of the three 'Map Select: ...' options: Taxpayer Only, Occupant Only, or Taxpayer & Occupant.
  2. Enter buffer distance.
  3. Select one of the draw tools (gray indicates selection) to mark the mailing label area on the map. Use the Esc key to start over during the drawing process.
      The buffer area will automatically appear on map when drawing is completed. Zoom out to see entire buffer area.
  4. Click the Execute button at bottom of panel to create report.

 Option 2 - By parcel number(s):
  1. Choose one of the two 'Select by Parcel Number: ...' options: Taxpayer Only or Taxpayer & Occupant.
  2. Type or paste assessor numbers into search box (separate values with a space or comma; quotes around numbers are ok):
  3. Enter buffer distance if desired, otherwise uncheck box for no buffer.
  4. Click the Execute button at bottom of panel to create report.

STEP 3: SAVE LABELS AND REPORT
  1. Mailing Labels - Click the Mailing Labels (click for CSV file) button near the top of panel to open a spreadsheet of mailing labels (no duplicates, only mailing fields), and then do a 'File Save As ...' on the resulting spreadsheet.
  2. Report - Click the '...' menu (upper right) and selecting the 'Export to CSV file' option to open a spreadsheet report of parcels selected (includes all records and fields), and then do a 'File Save As ...' on the resulting spreadsheet.

STEP 4: PRINT MAP WITH SELECTED ITEMS
  1. Click 'Layer List' button and turn on '*_Query Result' in the legend (may need to toggle off and on).
  2. Click 'Print' button to create printable document.

STEP 5: RUNNING ADDITIONAL REPORTS
  1. Select the 'Tasks' tab at top of panel.
  2. Redraw mailing label area, re-enter parcel numbers, or start over with a new task (STEP 2) by clicking the left arrow near top left of panel.
